    About Us

    We provide consumers with accurate information about average costs for medical procedures at the local, state and national levels. Medical costs in the United States can vary widely based on the medical facility being used, the medical procedure in question, and the insurance coverage that an individual has. Ideally, the medical industry would be more transparent and provide comprehensive cost data on medical procedures. Unfortunately, there are no signs that the status quo will change.

    Our mission is to proivde easy-to-access healthcare cost information that will empower consumers to made educated decisions when selecting doctors, insurance providers and medical care facilities.
